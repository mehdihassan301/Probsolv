How to Set Up a WhatsApp Business Store in 15 Minutes

WhatsApp Business lets you build a digital shop and share products directly in chat. Here’s how to set it up:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Download WhatsApp Business app from Play Store.
  2. Register your business number.
  3. Go to Settings > Business Tools > Catalog.
  4. Tap Add Product or Service.
  5. Upload images, price, and description.
  6. Save – your catalog is now ready to share!
